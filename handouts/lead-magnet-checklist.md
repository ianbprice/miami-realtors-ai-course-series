# Lead Magnet Launch Checklist

Use this checklist before publishing a Realtor lead magnet.

## 1. Pick the Audience

- Relocation buyer.
- Local buyer.
- Seller or homeowner.
- Second-home buyer.
- Investor.
- Past database contact.

## 2. Pick the Lead Magnet Type

- Five Coolest Things About Living in [Town].
- Local Guide to [Town].
- [Town] Questions Answered.
- Buyer checklist.
- Seller prep guide.
- Neighborhood comparison guide.

## 3. Build the Guide

- Keep it short and skimmable.
- Use real local knowledge.
- Add useful specifics.
- Include one clear soft CTA.
- Make the design clean enough to read on a phone.

## 4. Review Before Publishing

- Verify local facts.
- Remove invented stats.
- Remove risky Fair Housing language.
- Avoid demographic claims.
- Avoid school ranking claims.
- Avoid unsupported safety or crime claims.
- Confirm any images are licensed or created for use.

## 5. Capture the Lead

- Use a simple form.
- Ask only for the information you need.
- Deliver the guide immediately.
- Tag the lead source in your CRM.
- Record the guide topic they requested.

## 6. Follow Up

- Send a short thank-you message.
- Ask one simple question.
- Reference the guide they downloaded.
- Offer help without pushing for a call too early.
- Add them to the right nurture sequence.

## 7. Repurpose

- Turn each guide section into a social post.
- Turn one question into a short video.
- Turn the guide into an email.
- Turn the guide into a blog post.
- Revisit and refresh the guide monthly or quarterly.

## 8. Measure

- Views.
- Clicks.
- Form submissions.
- Replies.
- Appointments.
- Cost per lead, if using ads.
- Lead-to-appointment conversion.
