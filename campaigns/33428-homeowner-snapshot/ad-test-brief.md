# Paid Ad Test Brief

Paid ads are phase two only. Launch them only if organic content shows traction.

## Organic Threshold Before Paid

Run a paid test only if at least one of these happens:

- 3 or more form submissions.
- 10 or more meaningful clicks.
- Strong comment or DM activity around one hook.
- One post clearly outperforms the others in saves, shares, or profile visits.

## Compliance Rules

For Meta paid ads:

- Use Housing Special Ad Category.
- Do not target ZIP-only.
- Do not target by age, gender, income, family status, school preference, demographics, or protected-class proxies.
- Use broad Boca/West Boca geography allowed by Meta's current rules.
- Do not write copy that implies the viewer has a personal attribute.

Avoid:

- `Are you a homeowner in 33428?`
- `Thinking about selling your Boca home?`
- `Retired and ready to downsize?`

Prefer:

- `A quick guide for West Boca owners keeping an eye on the market.`
- `What 33428 sellers should review before pricing.`
- `Local context before a broad online estimate.`

## Budget

Starter test:

- $10-$20 per day.
- 7-10 days.
- One campaign.
- Three ad variations.
- One BoldTrail landing form.

## Decision After Test

After 7-10 days, keep only ads that produce:

- Click-through rate above the other variants.
- Form submissions at a tolerable cost.
- Replies or appointments from qualified local homeowners.

If there are clicks but no leads, fix the landing/form friction first. If there are impressions but no clicks, fix the hook or creative. If there are leads but no replies, improve follow-up speed and specificity.
