# 33428 Homeowner Snapshot Campaign Playbook

## Strategy

Start with homeowners in Boca Raton 33428 because Ian Burton Price can lead with true local credibility. The campaign should create low-friction seller conversations before someone has publicly raised their hand to sell.

Core message:

> Before you trust a broad online estimate, look at what buyers are actually comparing in 33428.

The campaign should sound like a local operator watching the market, not a salesperson asking for listings.

## Offer

Flagship lead magnet:

**33428 Homeowner Snapshot: What West Boca Owners Should Watch Before Selling**

Format:

- One-page PDF
- Gated by BoldTrail form
- Delivered immediately by email
- Followed by a short text/email sequence

Primary CTA:

> Want a property-specific 33428 snapshot?

Secondary CTA:

> Comment "33428" or message me and I will send the guide.

## Audience

Primary:

- Local homeowners in 33428
- People thinking about selling in the next 3 to 12 months
- Past clients, sphere contacts, neighbors, and local homeowners watching values

Secondary:

- Homeowners in nearby West Boca communities who identify with 33428 content
- People who own locally but may not live locally full time

## Organic Launch Flow

Week 1 builds authority and curiosity. Do not hard-launch the PDF yet.

Week 2 launches the PDF and pushes traffic to BoldTrail.

Week 3 educates sellers and gives people more reasons to request the guide.

Week 4 converts, follows up, reviews metrics, and decides whether a paid test is justified.

## Content Pillars

- Local resident credibility: Ian Burton Price lives in West Boca.
- Buyer behavior: what buyers notice before they make an offer.
- Seller prep: what to gather before pricing or listing.
- Market reality: local comps beat national headlines.
- Low-pressure help: a property-specific snapshot is available on request.

## Compliance Guardrails

- Do not claim a neighborhood is better, safer, or best for a protected class.
- Do not make school-ranking claims.
- Do not make unsupported safety, crime, demographic, religion, family-status, or disability claims.
- Do not imply the viewer personally owns, rents, earns a certain income, has kids, is retired, or belongs to a demographic group.
- Use verified MLS data before publishing any market-stat claim.
- Use Meta Housing Special Ad Category for any paid real estate ad.
