### Compliance Reminder Section

Headline:
AI can help draft. You still own the decision.

Body:
Before using AI-generated content with a client, lead, seller, buyer, public audience, MLS, or advertising channel, review it for accuracy, privacy, Fair Housing, brokerage policy, MLS rules, advertising rules, consumer communication rules, and professional judgment.

Short checklist:

- Verify facts, pricing, availability, market data, and property details.
- Do not paste confidential client or transaction information into public AI tools without authorization and safeguards.
- Avoid discriminatory targeting, protected-class language, steering language, and unsupported neighborhood claims.
- Follow brokerage, MLS, advertising, platform, and consumer communication requirements.
- Keep AI as support for drafting, organizing, and preparation. Do not treat it as final professional judgment.
