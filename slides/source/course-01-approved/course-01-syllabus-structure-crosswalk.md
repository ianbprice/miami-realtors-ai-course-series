## Cross-Check Result

No substantive original syllabus topic was removed. The revised syllabus reorganizes related material to match the current presentation sequence and the realistic two-hour delivery plan.

Automated term checks confirmed that each original syllabus topic is represented in the revised syllabus, the current deck text/notes, or both. Price reduction conversations are retained in the revised syllabus under "Listings, Marketing, and Appointment Preparation"; the current deck covers the related seller-pricing objection practice through the overpricing role-play and appointment preparation slides rather than using "price reduction" as a visible slide title.
